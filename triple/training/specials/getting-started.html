Getting Started - Special

A11Y Champions Training

First day - Focus

Understanding

What to do after awareness

A primary challenge to accomplish for accessibility is creating awareness. But what's next for the people aware? The first question people often have that we need to answer is "But how can I start with accessibility?"

Explore the problem from all angles:

  • Resources
  • Tutorials
  • Top 5
  • Low hanging fruit
  • What's right / wrong

Examples

Digital relieve

When dealing with a lot of people in large organisations and only a small accessibility team these questions should be answered digital to relieve the burden on the team.

  • Getting Started with Web Accessibility - W3C
  • Tips for Getting Started with Web Accessibility - W3C
  • Developing Accessible Websites - UOW
  • Getting Started with Accessibility - ING
  • Getting Started with Accessibility PDF posters - ING

Sketch

Break up in two or three groups and work out the following:

Essential Contents

Think about what should be part of the getting started. What is essential to know / use when beginning to make a difference?

  • For which people
  • From a design stand point
  • Do others know
  • Can I test it
  • How to check if it's right

Format

Which formats suits best for exposing people to and getting people start with accessibility?

  • Portal pages
  • Screen casts
  • Online learning course
  • Infographics
  • Top 10 Word documents

5 Minutes Break

Present

Sketch results

Each group presents their solutions to the other groups.

  • Essential content needed
  • Format of choice
  • Explain reasons why

In-Dept

Short break down of solutions

Together we challenge the solutions and sharpen the knife based on:

  • Customer needs
  • Employer needs
  • Team needs

Decide

Feedback processing

On basis of the feedback adjust the plan with your group and decide what can be delivered for a MVP.

  • Discuss and focus
  • Adjust sketch

Storyboarding

Prepare for propotyping

Next lessons we're going to work for an hour on a MVP prototype. Make a storyboard by organizing in the form of illustrations, sketches or images displayed in sequence for the purpose of pre-visualizing the MVP.

  • Create storyboard
  • Choose your tools
  • Prepare assets

Second day - Results

Prototype fase 1

Setup basics form

Build only what you need to validate your ideas in a very short time frame. Place all content, items and visuals in the prototype.

  • Experiment to test out a hypothesis
  • Essential elements of what you need
  • Take shortcuts

Prototype fase 2

Elaborate and finetune

Review the prototype and correct where needed.

  • Facade of experience from sketch phase
  • Short consultation meeting
  • Quick fix outcome

5 Minutes Break

Present

State the principles

Why have you done what you've done

  • Decisions on ideas
  • What was the concensus
  • What didn't make it

Validate

Moment of truth

Finally see the results with live users

  • The other group(s) interact with ideas
  • Feedback from the others

Conclusion

A short recap

The result of the 2 days workshop in summary.

  • Compilation of results
  • Reviews findings together
  • Actionable learnings

Next Steps

Discuss the follow ups needed

Actionable learnings that lead into the next round.

  • Run a follow up Sprint
  • Iterate and test again
  • Adjust current work present
  • Ready to implement

Congratulate and celebrate!
