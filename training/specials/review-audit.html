Review & Audit - Special

A11Y Champions Training

First day - Focus

Understanding

Executive Summary

The reports describe the Expert Review of the Digital 2.0 website and the conformance with W3C's Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0.

  • Review process
  • Meets VS Does not meet
  • Overall score
  • Comparison previous Review & Audit

Examples

Review and Audit Resources

The Review Process is based on the work of multiple W3C Task Forces, human interface and design guidelines from Apple, Google, BBC and more...

The Audits are based on the W3C's Conformance Evaluation method as described in Evaluating Web Sites for Accessibility.

Selection of guidelines:

  • Review & Audits ING NL
  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0
  • Techniques for Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0
  • Evaluating Web Sites for Accessibility
  • Material design Accessibility - Google
  • Human Interface Guidelines - Apple
  • Accessibility - Apple
  • Global Experience Guidelines - BBC
  • HTML Accessibility Standards - BBC

Sketch

High over discussion

Think and share what went right and what can be improved for the near future.

  • Effort invested
  • Dependencies
  • Not yet fixed
  • Progression
  • Root cause analysis

5 Minutes Break

Present

Top 20 Priority Issues

The most important issues to fix first according to the evaluator(s). These issues are based on the following:

  • Bug causing wrong result
  • Amount of people affected
  • Blocking for input methods or disability
  • Essential content or functionality
  • Difficulty to fix
  • How often it is used or occurs

In-Dept

Piece by piece treating

All top issues will be explained and discussed.

  • Top 20 list present
  • Working application
  • Reproduce issues
  • In-dept explanation

Decide

Break down of improvements

On basis of the review and audit adjust the plan for near future and decide what can be delivered.

  • Top 3 improvements
  • Plan of action

Second day - Results

Present

Choose your issues

Each champ has chosen two issues from the Review & Audit. These issues are from the squad of the champ or from the "ALL" section.

Chosen on basis of:

  • Specific open questions for the issue
  • Explain why it has not happened or failed

Validate

Break down of the issues

Together with the web site or application the champ will illustrate and explain the issue.

  • Root cause
  • Development or Design?
  • People or technology affected
  • Possible fixes

Discussion

Solution for fix

Together we'll discuss and decide what the best solution will be.

  • Technology specific
  • Accessibility supported
  • Temporarily or permanent

5 Minutes Break

Conclusion

What have we learned

The Expert Review & WCAG Audit is done, issues are run through and discussed. What have we learned so far.

  • Resume insight
